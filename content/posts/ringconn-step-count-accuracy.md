If a smart ring score feels “random”, it’s usually because the expectation is wrong.
Rings are not trying to give second-by-second truth. They’re trying to give trend-level insight with minimal friction.

## Why steps differ
- Phone steps depend on where you carry the phone
- Watch steps depend on wrist swing
- Ring steps depend on hand motion and sensor interpretation

## Who gets frustrated
People who treat steps as a precise scorecard. Steps are best as a trend, not as an exact total.

## Tiny honesty
If you push a stroller, carry groceries, or type all day, step algorithms behave differently. That’s normal.
