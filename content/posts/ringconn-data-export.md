Data export is one of those features you only care about after you’ve been tracking for weeks.
If it matters to you, check it before you buy, not after you’re emotionally invested.

## What to check before buying
- Does it connect to Apple Health / Google Fit in the way you want?
- Can you export summaries or raw-ish data?
- Are you okay if export is limited?

## Expectation calibration
Most rings are consumer wellness devices, not research tools.
If you want full raw data pipelines, you may be disappointed.
