People search firmware updates when something feels wrong. Sometimes updates help. Sometimes the best move is to stop poking the device and let it run.

This guide is about reducing update anxiety.

## The simple rule
Update when:
- you have a known bug that the update claims to fix
- you can keep the phone nearby for the whole process

Don’t update when:
- you’re traveling
- your ring is already working fine and you just feel curious

## Expectation calibration
A firmware update won’t turn a ring into a workout watch. If your complaint is “workout HR is wrong”, use a watch/strap.
