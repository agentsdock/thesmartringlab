If a smart ring score feels “random”, it’s usually because the expectation is wrong.
Rings are not trying to give second-by-second truth. They’re trying to give trend-level insight with minimal friction.

## The common misunderstanding
Many users expect the app to behave like a live fitness watch.
Smart rings are usually optimized for trend collection, not second-by-second feedback.

## What is normal
- One daily sync fills in sleep and recovery summaries
- Data appears later than you expect

## What is not normal
- The ring never syncs for days
- The app can’t see the ring at all

## The best habit
Sync once in the morning. Check trends weekly. Stop refreshing the app all day.
