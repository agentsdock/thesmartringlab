If you own a RingConn Gen 1, Gen 2, or Gen 2 Air, you can get up to $70 off when upgrading to Gen 3.

## Trade-in discount amounts

| Current ring | Maximum trade-in discount |
|--------------|---------------------------|
| RingConn Gen 1 | $70 |
| RingConn Gen 2 | $70 |
| RingConn Gen 2 Air | $50 |

**Combined with pre-order:**
- Gen 3 pre-order: $314
- Trade-in: up to $70 off
- **Minimum price: $244** (Gen 2 owner + pre-order + max trade-in)

## How the trade-in works

**Step 1: Check eligibility**

You need proof of purchase for your current RingConn ring:
- Order confirmation email
- RingConn account showing your registered device
- Original receipt

**Step 2: Apply during Gen 3 purchase**

The trade-in discount is applied at checkout when you order Gen 3. You don't need to send your old ring back.

**Step 3: Keep your old ring**

RingConn doesn't require you to return the old device. This is different from some trade-in programs.

## What to do with your old ring

Since you keep it, options include:
- **Backup ring**: Keep it as a spare if Gen 3 has issues
- **Family member**: Give it to someone who wants to try smart rings
- **Compare data**: Wear both rings for a week to compare accuracy
- **Sell secondhand**: Gen 2 still has resale value (~$150-200 on eBay)

## Is the trade-in worth it?

**Worth it if:**
- You were planning to upgrade anyway
- Your Gen 2 battery is degraded (common after 1-2 years)
- You want the new features (vibration, BP trends, longer battery)

**Not worth it if:**
- Your Gen 2 works perfectly and you don't need new features
- You're sensitive to budget ($244 minimum is still significant)
- You just bought Gen 2 recently (less than 6 months)

## Important caveats

1. **Sizing doesn't transfer**: Gen 3 uses a new sizing system. Even with trade-in, you need the free sizing kit.

2. **Trade-in value varies**: The "up to $70" means the actual discount depends on your specific ring model and purchase history.

3. **Limited time**: Trade-in program details may change after pre-order period ends.
