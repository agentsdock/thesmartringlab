The goal of a charging case is not “cool accessory”, it’s to remove charging friction.
If you still forget to charge, you don’t have a battery problem, you have a workflow problem.

## The two routines that actually work
1) Shower routine: charge while you shower, twice a week.
2) Desk routine: keep the case next to your keyboard and charge during the same meeting time.

## Tiny details that reduce friction
- Keep the case where you already have a habit (bathroom counter, desk), not in a drawer.
- Don’t “top up” daily. Daily charging makes it feel like a watch.
- If you travel, decide whether the ring is worth packing the case. Many users don’t.
