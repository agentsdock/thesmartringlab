If a smart ring score feels “random”, it’s usually because the expectation is wrong.
Rings are not trying to give second-by-second truth. They’re trying to give trend-level insight with minimal friction.

## What it can help with
- Noticing trend-level changes that may correlate with illness, stress, or cycle patterns

## What it can’t do
- Replace a thermometer for medical decisions
- Give reliable meaning from one night change

## How to use it without overreacting
- Look at weekly trend
- Combine with sleep duration and resting HR trend
