Most buyers don’t care about “maximum battery life” on paper. They care about one thing: will this device become another daily chore?

This guide focuses on battery as *workflow*, not as a marketing spec.

## What usually drains battery faster
- Frequent app checking and repeated syncing attempts
- More frequent sensor sampling modes (if available)
- Lots of Bluetooth reconnect cycles

## The lowest-friction charging routine
- Pick two fixed days per week to charge (example: Tue + Fri).
- Charge during a predictable time block (shower or desk time).
- Avoid “charge panic” after you notice low battery.

## Expectation calibration
Rings are designed to be worn passively. If you treat the app like a live dashboard, you’ll sync more often and feel more battery anxiety.
