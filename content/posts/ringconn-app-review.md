The ring is only half the product. The app decides whether you feel “insight” or “random charts”.
This page is about the practical experience: what feels clear, and what causes frustration.

## What the app tends to do well
- Summaries that reward consistency (sleep/recovery trends)
- Simple daily check-in workflow (if you keep it simple)

## What frustrates people
- Expecting the app to update like a live watch
- Interpreting early readiness scores as “truth” without baseline
- Obsessing over sleep stages instead of trends

## The best workflow (low-friction)
- Sync once in the morning
- Look at 7-day trends
- Use notes (stressful day, late meal) to explain changes
