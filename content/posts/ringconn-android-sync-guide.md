Using a smart ring on Android is straightforward when expectations are correct: you wear it, you sync once per day, you look at trends.

Friction usually starts when people expect real-time dashboards.

## Expectation calibration
- Syncing is often batch-based, not live.
- A single daily sync is a feature, not a bug, it reduces obsession and battery drain.

## Common friction patterns
- Permission toggles (battery optimization, Bluetooth permissions)
- “It didn’t update instantly” panic-refresh loops

## Fix checklist (fast)
1) Disable aggressive battery optimization for the app
2) Re-check Bluetooth permissions
3) Sync once, then wait
