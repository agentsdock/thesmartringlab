If your RingConn looks “wrong”, don’t assume the ring is broken. Most issues come from a few repeatable causes: fit, syncing habits, and expectations about real-time feedback.

This page is designed for the search intent behind “RingConn Bluetooth connection issues: what’s normal, what’s not”: you want a fast fix that works.

## What you’ll usually notice
- The ring disconnects and reconnects often
- The app can’t find the ring
- Syncing works only when the app is open

## The fastest path to a fix (in order)
### Fix 1
Calibrate expectations: rings often sync in batches. You don’t need a permanent live connection.

### Fix 2
Re-check permissions and battery optimization settings.

### Fix 3
Forget the device in Bluetooth settings, then re-pair from inside the app (clean pairing).


## What is normal vs what is not
- Normal: occasional sync lag, data filling in after a single daily sync.
- Not normal: the ring never syncs across multiple days, or the app cannot see the device at all.
