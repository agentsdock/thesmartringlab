## What you get with pre-order

- The same ring as regular buyers
- Early access to blood pressure trend feature (already in beta for Gen 2 users)

## What you risk with pre-order

**Risk 1: No full reviews yet**

Gen 3 launched at CES 2026 (January). Full reviews from major outlets (PCMag, Tom's Guide, etc.) are still coming in.

If you care about detailed battery testing, accuracy comparisons, or long-term wear reports, you might want to wait.

**Risk 2: Blood pressure feature is still evolving**

The nighttime blood pressure trend feature is new. RingConn has been testing it with Gen 2 users in beta, but the Gen 3 implementation may have differences.

If you need this feature for medical reasons, wait for more clinical validation.

**Risk 3: Sizing system confusion**

Gen 3 uses a new sizing system. If you order based on your Gen 2 size, you might get the wrong fit.

Order the free sizing kit. Don't guess.

## Who should pre-order now

**Pre-order if:**
- You were already planning to buy RingConn (the $35 discount is real savings)
- You own Gen 1 or Gen 2 and want the trade-in discount
- You travel often and need the 17-day battery
- You're curious about blood pressure trends and willing to be an early adopter

**Wait if:**
- You want full reviews from trusted sources
- You need the blood pressure feature for medical monitoring (wait for clinical validation)
- You're budget-sensitive and Gen 2 at $299 is attractive enough
- You don't care about vibration alerts or BP trends
