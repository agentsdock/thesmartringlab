Smart rings live on your hand. That means they take hits from desks, weights, doors, and daily friction.
Many buyers underestimate how quickly cosmetic wear appears.

## What is normal
- Light micro-scratches over time
- Wear marks from desks and keyboards

## What’s avoidable
- Heavy scratches from weightlifting, dumbbells, barbells
- Metal-on-metal contact (tools, gym equipment)

## Tiny honesty
Some people discover they simply don’t like wearing rings all day. That’s not a product failure, it’s a lifestyle mismatch.
