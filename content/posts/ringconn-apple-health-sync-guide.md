If you’re using an iPhone, Apple Health is the “single dashboard” people want. The frustration is that rings don’t always behave like a live watch.

This guide calibrates expectations, then gives a simple troubleshooting order.

## Expectation calibration
It’s normal for ring data to appear with delay. Rings are optimized for passive trend collection, not second-by-second live updates.

## What usually syncs
- Sleep summaries
- Heart rate (often as periodic samples)
- Activity summaries (varies)

## What people expect but often don’t get
- Perfect, instant updates after every movement
- Identical metrics compared to a watch

## A simple troubleshooting order
1) Confirm permissions inside iOS Health settings
2) Do one manual sync inside the RingConn app
3) Wait, then check again, don’t refresh every minute
