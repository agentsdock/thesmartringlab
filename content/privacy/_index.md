## Last updated: May 2026

## What data we collect

**We don't collect personal data directly.**

This site is a static website. We don't have user accounts, login forms, or newsletter signups (yet).

If you email us, we keep your message for reference. We don't share it with third parties.

## Third-party services

This site may use:

- **Analytics** (if enabled): Basic traffic stats (pages visited, referrer, country). No personal identification.
- **Affiliate links**: Links to products may include tracking codes. If you click and buy, the retailer knows you came from here. We don't see your purchase details.
- **CDN/hosting**: The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may log basic request data for security and performance.

## Cookies

If analytics is enabled, a cookie may be set to distinguish users. It's a first-party cookie, used only for aggregate stats.

You can block cookies in your browser settings. The site still works.

## Affiliate disclosure

Some links to products are affiliate links. If you click and purchase, we may earn a commission. This doesn't affect what we write. We don't accept sponsored reviews.

## Changes

If we add newsletter signups or other features, we'll update this policy.
