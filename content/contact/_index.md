## What we respond to

- Corrections if something is wrong or outdated
- Questions about specific articles (we may add your question to a future update)
- Suggestions for topics we should cover

## What we don't do

- Sponsored content or paid reviews
- Affiliate pressure (we link to products, but we don't let commissions change what we write)
- Brand PR requests to "fix" negative coverage

If a product has real downsides for certain users, we keep them in the guide.
